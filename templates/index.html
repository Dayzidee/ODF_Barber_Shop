Your Premium At-Home Barber Experience

Simple steps to enjoy professional grooming in your own space. Plus, see if you're in our service zone!

The Process: Easy & Convenient

  1. 1. Book Your Slot: Use our easy online form to select your services, preferred date, time, and provide your location.
  2. 2. Confirmation Call/Text: We'll contact you shortly after your request to confirm the exact appointment time and answer any questions.
  3. 3. Barber Arrives: Your professional barber arrives at your doorstep, fully equipped and ready to provide a top-tier service.
  4. 4. Relax & Get Styled: Sit back in your comfortable environment while we craft your perfect look. We handle the setup and cleanup.
  5. 5. Look Great, Feel Great: Step out with a fresh, confident style, without ever leaving your home!

What We Need From You:

  • A comfortable chair for you to sit in.
  • A well-lit area (natural light is great, but good indoor lighting works too).
  • Access to a power outlet (for clippers, etc.).
  • Clear a small space for the barber to work (approx. 5ft x 5ft).
